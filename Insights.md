# Insights Report
## Bengaluru Luxury Housing Market Intelligence — FY 2023-25

---

## 1. Market Overview

- **Total Listings Analyzed:** 100,000
- **Total Revenue:** ₹12,65,225 Cr
- **Average Ticket Price:** ₹12.65 Cr
- **Median Ticket Price:** ₹12.04 Cr
- **Average Price Per Sqft:** ₹22,917
- **NRI Buyer Share:** 50.0%
- **Time Period Covered:** Q2 2023 to Q1 2025 (8 quarters)

## 3. Key Insights

### 3.2 Configuration Demand

| Configuration | Count | Share |
|--------------|------:|------:|
| 5BHK+ | 33,536 | 33.5% |
| 3BHK | 33,377 | 33.4% |
| 4BHK | 33,087 | 33.1% |

**Insight:** Demand is almost equally split across all three configurations. In the luxury segment, even 5BHK+ units have strong demand, unlike the mid-range market where 2-3BHK dominates. This tells us Bangalore's luxury buyers are willing to pay for larger spaces.

### 3.3 Pricing Segmentation

| Price Tier | Count | Share |
|-----------|------:|------:|
| Premium (₹10-15 Cr) | 62,570 | 62.6% |
| Mid Luxury (₹5-10 Cr) | 21,512 | 21.5% |
| Ultra Premium (₹15-20 Cr) | 13,713 | 13.7% |
| Super Luxury (₹20+ Cr) | 1,316 | 1.3% |
| Entry Luxury (₹0-5 Cr) | 889 | 0.9% |

**Insight:** The ₹10-15 Cr "Premium" segment is the sweet spot, accounting for nearly 63% of all transactions. The ₹20+ Cr Super Luxury segment is niche at just 1.3% — these represent the highest outlier properties. Entry luxury below ₹5 Cr is minimal, confirming this dataset genuinely captures the luxury segment.

### 3.6 Sales Channel Performance

| Sales Channel | Count | Share |
|--------------|------:|------:|
| Online | 25,169 | 25.2% |
| Broker | 25,124 | 25.1% |
| NRI Desk | 25,032 | 25.0% |
| Direct | 24,675 | 24.7% |

**Insight:** Sales channels are nearly equally effective. Online and Broker channels lead marginally. The fact that Direct sales (no middleman) contribute almost 25% indicates strong brand awareness among builders. NRI Desk handles a quarter of all transactions, aligning with the 50% NRI buyer share.

### 3.8 Buyer Sentiment

| Sentiment | Count | Share |
|----------|------:|------:|
| Positive | 45,548 | 45.5% |
| Negative | 27,453 | 27.5% |
| Neutral | 26,999 | 27.0% |

**Top Positive Comments:** "Great value for money", "Excellent location!", "Loved the amenities!", "Underpriced for location"

**Top Negative Comments:** "Connectivity is poor", "Agent was not responsive", "Too far from my office"

**Insight:** Nearly half of all buyer comments are positive, which is a healthy sign for the market. Among negative comments, "Connectivity is poor" and "Too far from my office" point to location/infrastructure concerns. "Agent was not responsive" highlights a service quality gap in the broker channel that developers should address.
